Pizza

They say circle is the perfect shape...and therefore the perfect food couldn't have a different one!

Pizza, the worldwide-famous dish..Anywhere you are in this moment, rest assured you will be able to find a restaurant that makes it. But let's see how we could cook it at home!

Ingredients

(for 3 pizzas)
  • 200 gr mnitoba flour
  • 300 gr flour 00
  • 300 ml water
  • yeast 1tsp
  • fine salt 2tsp
  • 400 gr tomatoe sauce
  • 250 gr mozzarella
  • fresh basil
  • olive oil
  • salt

Instructions

For the dough:

  1. Place both flours, yeast and small part of water in a bowls
  2. Stir with a spoon
  3. Gradually add water
  4. Knead by hand until the dough is smooth and homogeneous
  5. Leave it to rest 10 minutes
  6. Fold it (imagine it's divided it in 4 parts)
  7. Shape into a ball
  8. Place into a bowl, cover with plastic wrap and let leaven (~6hours)
  9. After leavining time is up, divide the dough in 3 parts with a spatula
  10. For each portion take the edge and lift it upwards
  11. Repeat for all other 3 edges
  12. Round the dough until a regular ball is made
  13. Place each piece on a pizza tray, cover with lid and rest 30 minutes
  14. Stretch the dough...
  15. ...it's now ready to be topped!

For the topping:

  1. Pre-heat the oven at 250Â°C
  2. pass the tomatoe sauce onto the stretched dough
  3. cut the mozzarella, and add it
  4. Add the salt and the oil
  5. Add the pizza to the oven. After no more than 5 minutes it's ready!
Enjoy your pizza!
